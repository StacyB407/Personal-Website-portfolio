Delicious Pilau

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice (basmati preferred)
  • 1 lb beef, chicken, or goat meat (optional), cubed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated or minced
  • 2-3 tomatoes, chopped (or 14.5 oz canned)
  • 1-2 carrots, diced (optional)
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced (optional)
  • 1/2 cup green peas (optional)
  • 4 cups beef, chicken, or vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp cardamom pods
  • 1/2 tsp cloves
  • 1/2 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley, chopped (garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot, add cumin seeds, cardamom, cloves, peppercorns, cinnamon. Cook 1 min.
  2. Add onion, cook until golden. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 min.
  3. If using meat, add and brown on all sides.
  4. Add tomatoes, cook until broken down. Add carrots, bell pepper, peas, cook few mins.
  5. Add rice, stir to coat. Pour in broth, bring to boil, add salt.
  6. Reduce heat, cover, simmer 20-25 mins until rice is cooked.
  7. Remove from heat, let rest 10 mins covered. Fluff with fork.
  8. Garnish with cilantro or parsley. Serve hot.
